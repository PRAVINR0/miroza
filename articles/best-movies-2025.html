Best Movies of the Year 2025

By Entertainment Desk | Updated: Dec 06, 2025, 14:30 IST

2025 has been a stellar year for cinema, with groundbreaking films across all genres. From sci-fi epics to intimate dramas, filmmakers have pushed the boundaries of storytelling and visual effects.

Top on our list is "The Last Horizon," a space odyssey that has captivated audiences worldwide. Its stunning visuals and emotional depth make it a must-watch.

Another standout is "Echoes of Silence," a psychological thriller that keeps you on the edge of your seat. The performances are top-notch, and the screenplay is masterful.
