Global Food Prices Stabilize After Five Months of Volatility

By MIROZA Economic Desk • Jan 8, 2025 • Category: Economy

Stability Drivers

New agricultural trade agreements and improved crop forecasts have helped stabilize global food prices. Economists believe that supply chain strengthening played a major role.

Ongoing Risks

However, the UN warns that climate-driven risks continue to threaten long-term food security.
