Major Breakthrough Achieved in Fusion Energy Reactor

By MIROZA Science Bureau • Jan 10, 2025 • Category: Science

Research Milestone

Scientists at the International Fusion Research Facility announced sustained net-positive fusion output for over 12 minutes, a world-record achievement that brings fusion power closer to mainstream viability.

Technical Leap

This breakthrough used advanced magnetic confinement techniques and next-generation superconductive materials. The scientific community hailed the results as a revolutionary leap toward limitless clean energy.
