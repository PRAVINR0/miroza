A Guide to Building Sustainable Cities

By Maria Gonzalez | Updated: Dec 06, 2025

COPENHAGEN â€” As urbanization accelerates, the need for sustainable cities has never been more critical. But what makes a city truly sustainable?

It starts with energy. Cities like Copenhagen are leading the way with district heating systems powered by renewable energy and waste-to-energy plants.

Transportation is another key pillar. Prioritizing pedestrians, cyclists, and public transit over private cars reduces emissions and improves public health. "The 15-minute city concept, where everything you need is within a short walk or bike ride, is the gold standard," explains urban planner Lars Jensen.

Finally, green spaces are essential. Vertical gardens, rooftop parks, and urban forests not only beautify the city but also cool it down and improve air quality.
