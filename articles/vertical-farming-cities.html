The Rise of Vertical Farming: Feeding Cities from Within

By David Chen | Updated: Dec 06, 2025, 11:00 IST

By 2050, nearly 70% of the world's population will live in urban areas. Feeding these dense populations without destroying the planet requires a radical rethink of agriculture. Enter vertical farming.

In converted warehouses and purpose-built skyscrapers across Tokyo, New York, and London, crops are being grown in stacked layers, using 95% less water than traditional farming and zero pesticides.

This isn't science fiction. It's a rapidly growing industry that promises fresh, local produce year-round, regardless of weather conditions.
