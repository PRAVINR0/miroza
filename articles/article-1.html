How— MIROZA Structures Modern Content

By Editorial Team • Jan 2, 2025 • Category: Articles

MIROZA's content architecture emphasizes separation of concerns: data in JSON, presentation via semantic HTML, styling through modern CSS, and progressive enhancement with modular JavaScript under a single namespace.

Performance is improved by lazy loading media, prefetching navigational intent, and caching core assets through a service worker. Accessibility remains integral through logical heading structure, focus management, and careful contrast ratios.

Security considerations include strict Content Security Policy, avoidance of unsafe dynamic HTML insertion, and support for sanitization with libraries such as DOMPurify when user-generated content becomes necessary.
