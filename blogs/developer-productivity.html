10 Tips for Developer Productivity in 2025

Dec 05, 2025 | 5 min read

Staying productive as a developer is harder than ever. With new frameworks releasing every week and AI tools vying for our attention, it's easy to get distracted. Here are my top 10 tips to stay focused and ship code faster.

1. Master Your IDE

Whether you use VS Code, IntelliJ, or Fleet, knowing your shortcuts is non-negotiable. Spend an hour this week learning 5 new shortcuts. It pays off.

2. Embrace AI Pair Programmers

Tools like GitHub Copilot are not here to replace you; they are here to remove the drudgery. Use them for boilerplate, tests, and documentation.

3. Deep Work Sessions

Block out 2-3 hours of uninterrupted time. Turn off Slack notifications. Put your phone in another room. This is where the magic happens.
