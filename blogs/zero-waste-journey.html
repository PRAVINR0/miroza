My Journey to Zero Waste Living: One Year Later

By Maya Patel | Updated: Dec 06, 2025, 09:00 IST

It started with a simple goal: fit all my trash for a year into a single mason jar. Spoiler alert: I didn't quite make it, but the journey changed my life.

Transitioning to a zero-waste lifestyle isn't about perfection; it's about making better choices every day. From swapping plastic toothbrushes for bamboo ones to buying in bulk, small changes add up.

The hardest part? Grocery shopping. But once I found local markets that allowed me to bring my own containers, it became a joy rather than a chore.
