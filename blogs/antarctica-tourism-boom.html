Hidden Gems of Antarctica: Tourism Booms

By Marcus Thorne | Updated: Dec 06, 2025

Once reserved for scientists and extreme explorers, Antarctica has become the hottest travel destination of 2025. Luxury eco-cruises now offer comfortable access to the White Continent.

I spent two weeks exploring the Antarctic Peninsula. Kayaking among icebergs the size of skyscrapers and witnessing penguin colonies firsthand is a humbling experience that changes your perspective on our planet's fragility.

Strict environmental regulations ensure that tourism remains sustainable, but the demand is skyrocketing.
