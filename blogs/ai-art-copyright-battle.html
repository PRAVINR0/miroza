The AI Art Copyright Battle: Who Owns Creativity?

By Legal Eagle | Updated: Dec 06, 2025

If an AI paints a masterpiece, who owns it? The programmer? The user? Or the millions of artists whose work trained the algorithm?

Courts around the world are grappling with this question as artists file class-action lawsuits against major AI labs. The outcome will define the future of copyright law in the digital age.

"It's not about stopping technology," says one plaintiff. "It's about fair compensation for the human labor that makes it possible."
