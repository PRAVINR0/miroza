Sensex and Nifty Open Flat Amid Global Cues

Published Nov 25, 2025

Advertisement

IT and Banking Counters Lead Moves

The Sensex traded within a 120-point range during the opening half hour while the Nifty50 clung to the 21,800 mark. IT majors, select banks, and oil marketing companies saw light buying, offsetting profit-taking in auto and metal names.

Dealers said traders are reluctant to add aggressive positions ahead of Thursday's GDP reading and monthly derivatives expiry. The India VIX index stayed muted near 12, indicating limited hedging demand.

Global Picture Remains Mixed

European futures pointed to a soft open while Asian peers delivered a patchy handover, with Japanese shares slipping even as Chinese indices gained. US Treasury yields steadied overnight, providing some respite for emerging market flows.

Brokerages advised clients to use intraday dips to accumulate quality midcaps tied to investment and infrastructure themes. They expect earnings upgrades in December if government spending remains on track through the election cycle.
