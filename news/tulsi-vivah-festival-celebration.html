Tulsi Vivah Celebrations Across India

Published Nov 25, 2025

Rituals and Community Kitchens

Families in Maharashtra, Gujarat, and Uttar Pradesh conducted symbolic weddings of tulsi plants with Lord Vishnu idols, complete with mangalsutra, bangles, and traditional bhajans. Community kitchens distributed jaggery sweets and khichdi to visitors.

Temples in Varanasi livestreamed the rituals for diaspora devotees, while local administrations arranged crowd control and sanitation services late into the night.

Start of the Wedding Season

Pandits reminded devotees that Tulsi Vivah marks the beginning of the auspicious wedding calendar. Wedding planners reported a spike in bookings for December and January, with many couples opting for eco-friendly ceremonies.

Artisans selling brass diyas, clay ornaments, and handwoven textiles saw brisk business at festive markets, providing a boost to local livelihoods.
