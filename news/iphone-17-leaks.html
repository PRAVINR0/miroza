New iPhone 17 Leaks Reveal Holographic Display

By Tech Insider | Updated: Dec 06, 2025, 13:15 IST

The latest leaks from the supply chain suggest that Apple's upcoming iPhone 17 will feature a revolutionary holographic display technology. This would allow users to view 3D content without the need for special glasses.

Insiders claim that the new display, dubbed "Retina Holo," uses a multi-layered screen to project light fields directly into the user's eyes. If true, this would be the biggest leap in smartphone display technology since the original Retina display.

Other rumored features include a solid-state battery with a week-long life and a completely portless design.
