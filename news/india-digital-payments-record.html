India Sets New Digital Payments Record

Published Nov 25, 2025

UPI Crosses 15 Billion Transactions

NPCI data shows UPI processed 15.2 billion payments worth ₹25.6 lakh crore in October, an 18% jump month-on-month. Tier-II cities accounted for 43% of new QR code activations as kirana stores offered instant cashback schemes.

BHIM, PhonePe, and Google Pay retained the top spots, while banks reported faster settlement cycles thanks to upgraded cloud infrastructure.

RuPay, NETC FASTag Lead Offline Growth

RuPay credit on UPI saw a 32% surge in festive shopping, and NETC FASTag collections touched ₹100 crore as highway travel increased. Metro rail operators in Delhi and Bengaluru expanded contactless gates to cut queueing time.

Fintech executives expect the upcoming UPI Lite X feature and offline tap transactions to boost adoption in rural bazaars ahead of the year-end wedding rush.
