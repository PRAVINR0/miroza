Ayodhya Ram Mandir Inauguration Preparations Intensify

Published Nov 25, 2025

Security and Logistics

The Uttar Pradesh government deployed additional police units and installed AI-enabled surveillance cameras around the temple complex. Drone patrols will monitor crowds expected to exceed 500,000 visitors over inauguration week.

Railway authorities added special trains from Lucknow, Varanasi, and New Delhi, while airlines announced extra charter slots to nearby airports. Hotels and homestays reported full bookings, prompting the tourism department to set up temporary accommodations.

Community Participation

Local artisans are finishing sandstone carvings and frescoes while women's self-help groups prepare prasad packets. Cultural troupes from 12 states will perform Ramayana-themed tableaux during the procession.

Officials said inauguration rehearsals begin next week and requested visitors to pre-register through the dedicated portal to manage footfall. The Prime Minister is expected to lead the consecration ceremony, followed by a public address near the Sarayu riverfront.
