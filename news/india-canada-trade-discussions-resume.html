India–Canada Trade Discussions Resume

Published Nov 25, 2025

Focus on Practical Deliverables

Trade officials confirmed that working groups restarted discussions on a comprehensive economic partnership after a year-long pause. Sources said both sides prioritized tariff relief for agricultural shipments, clean-technology collaboration, and simplified compliance norms for midsized exporters.

Canada sought more predictable visa processing for students and professionals, while India pressed for assurances on supply chain diversification and data localization policies.

Timeline and Next Steps

The delegations agreed to meet again in January to review draft chapters covering digital trade and dispute settlement. Business chambers welcomed the resumption, saying greater clarity would help companies plan cross-border investments.
