RBI Report Highlights Strong Investment Climate

Published Nov 25, 2025

Capex Pipelines Stay Healthy

The RBI said new project announcements touched a two-year high in October, with engineering and construction firms booking long-term orders across highways, renewable energy, and city transit projects. Bankers reported that working capital utilization remains below pre-pandemic levels, giving companies room to borrow for expansion.

The bulletin showed that bank credit to industry grew 8.9% year-on-year, while services recorded a double-digit expansion, aided by logistics players investing in warehousing and fulfillment networks.

Policy Support Continues

Economists reading the report believe the central bank will continue to prioritize price stability while signaling comfort with the current pace of growth. The RBI reiterated that it stands ready with liquidity tools to cushion any external shocks as advanced economies slow.

Industry associations welcomed the findings, saying they reinforce boardroom confidence just as election-linked spending picks up. Several manufacturing CEOs told— MIROZA they intend to front-load investments before borrowing costs rise again.
