India’s Economy Grows 8.2% in Q2, Beats Forecasts

MIROZA Economy Desk — Nov 28, 2025

India’s economy posted a strong 8.2% year-on-year growth in the July–September quarter of 2025, marking one of the fastest quarterly growth rates in recent years. Strong consumer demand, robust export front-loading ahead of global tariff pressures, and stimulus-driven infrastructure spending contributed significantly to the growth.

Manufacturing and construction sectors reported notable gains of 9.1% and 7.2% respectively, while private consumption rose by 7.9%. Analysts noted that despite global headwinds and trade tensions, domestic demand and policy support helped sustain momentum.

Economic experts believe that if structural reforms continue and external pressures ease, India could maintain 6–7% growth through FY26. However, risks remain: inflation control, global trade uncertainties, and credit flow to SMEs will be key to watch.
