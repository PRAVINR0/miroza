Global Tech Summit 2025: Leaders Pledge $500 Billion for Green Tech

Miroza News Network | Updated: Dec 06, 2025, 08:30 IST

SAN FRANCISCO: In a historic move, leaders from the world's top 50 technology companies have pledged a combined $500 billion towards sustainable technology initiatives over the next five years. The announcement came during the opening keynote of the Global Tech Summit 2025.

The "Green Tech Accord" aims to accelerate the development of carbon-capture technologies, next-generation batteries, and fusion energy research. "We are not just building products; we are building a future," said the CEO of TechGiant Corp.

Key Highlights:

  • Commitment to 100% renewable energy for all data centers by 2027.
  • Launch of the "Open Earth" data platform for climate scientists.
  • New scholarship programs for students in environmental engineering.

The summit continues tomorrow with sessions on Quantum Computing and Space Exploration.
