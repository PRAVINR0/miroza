Crypto Market Surge: Bitcoin Hits New High

By Finance Team | Updated: Dec 06, 2025, 11:30 IST

Bitcoin has shattered all previous records, trading above $150,000 for the first time in history. The surge is attributed to widespread institutional adoption and the approval of several new crypto ETFs in major Asian markets.

Analysts predict that this bull run is different from previous cycles, citing the maturity of the market and the involvement of traditional financial giants. "Crypto is no longer a fringe asset class," says John Smith, CEO of Global Investments.

Ethereum and Solana have also seen significant gains, with the total crypto market cap now exceeding $5 trillion.
