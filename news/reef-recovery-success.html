Great Barrier Reef Shows Signs of Full Recovery

By Eco Watch | Updated: Dec 06, 2025

QUEENSLAND â€” In a stunning turnaround, marine biologists report that the Great Barrier Reef has regained 90% of its coral cover, thanks to a decade of aggressive conservation efforts and new "super-coral" seeding techniques.

The breakthrough came from a combination of cooling the local waters using renewable energy and introducing heat-resistant coral strains bred in labs. "We thought we had lost it forever," said Dr. Maria Santos. "Nature is more resilient than we give it credit for, if we just give it a chance."

Tourism operators are celebrating the news, expecting a record number of eco-tourists this season.
