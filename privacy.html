Privacy memo

Reader privacy stays default-on

These notes summarize how MIROZA treats audience insights, telemetry, and contact data across newsletters, quick-find interactions, and article engagement.

Collection

  • Only aggregated analytics are stored; IPs are truncated before leaving the edge.
  • No third-party pixels outside of self-hosted analytics and error tracking.
  • Newsletter opt-ins persist under miroza_newsletter_optin solely for rate-limiting.

Usage

  • Signals inform editorial prioritization and never resell user profiles.
  • Quick-find recents/saves remain inside the browser via localStorage keys.
  • Deletion requests are honored within 5 business days via privacy@miroza.online.

Compliance

  • DPIA templates mirror this memo for EU/UK regimes.
  • Regional partners receive change logs before policy updates go live.
  • We document vendors and retention schedules for SOC 2 / ISO mapping.
