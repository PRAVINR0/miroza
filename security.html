Security

At Miroza, we are committed to protecting the security of your personal information. We use a variety of security technologies and procedures to help protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure.

Secure Data Transmission

We use Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) technology to encrypt data transmitted between your browser and our servers. This ensures that any information you send to us, such as login credentials or payment details, remains private and secure.

Data Storage

Your personal information is stored on secure servers that are protected by firewalls and other security measures. We limit access to your personal information to those employees and contractors who need to know that information to provide services to you.

Reporting Security Issues

If you believe you have discovered a security vulnerability in our website or services, please contact us immediately at security@miroza.com. We take all security reports seriously and will investigate them promptly.
